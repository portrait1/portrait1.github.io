Craps
Unlike baccarat, playing craps can be really confusing at first. That's why playing craps for free online is the perfect way to learn all the rules without any of the risks. Each round starts with what's called a “come-out roll”. Most players bet on the “pass line” and if a 7 or 11 is rolled those bets win. If a 2, 3 or 12 hits, aka “craps”, pass line bets lose. If any other number is rolled it becomes the “point” number. Pass line bets win if the point number is rolled before a 7 hits. When the point or a 7 is rolled, a new round begins with a new come-out roll. This is just the start of what's available to bet on in craps, not to mention the strategies and systems you can use to improve your odds of winning.

Similar to blackjack, it pays to learn proper strategy when you're playing video poker. If you learn to use the best strategies you can lower the house edge to less than 1%.
